Useful Links

You can't just invest on your own simple knowledge. Below are some incredible resources that even the proffesionals use!

  • Tradermob
  • FINVIZ
  • Investopedia
  • Yahoo Finance
  • Google Finance
Tradermob

Tradermob is the location of our stock competition!Along with real-time portfolio trackers and competitions are plenty of incredibly useful tutorials and lessons, along with forums.

FINVIZ

FINVIZ is the absolute best stock screener out there. You can look up a bunch of tickers and get insane amounts of data and compare the stocks in indexes!

Investopedia

Investopedia is yet another wallstreetsurvivor. It too has an incredible amount of stock resources that literally anyone can understand and learn from. They even have their own portfolio trackers and competitions!

Yahoo Finance

Yahoo Finance has interactive charts, news, real-time quotes, embeddable resources, historical prices, crazy amounts of data, and so much more. It is the absolute most dependable resource for getting any stock information out there.

Google Finance

Google Finance is an excellent alternative to Yahoo Finance. Along with an incredible, simple look, tons of data is very easy to access everywhere in it.

Stocktwits

Stocktwits is the best way to find out what stocks people are talking about, along with finding some not-so-smart investors
