Competition

Stock Market Club is competing in an intense competition on tradermob.com with paper money. The contest is to end at the end of the semester and the person with the most return wins prizes!

How to Join

1. Make an account on Tradermob

2. Click on the contests link on the left and click the "Mobbers" tag

3. Search for the Fairview High School competition and request a join.

4. After being approved, start trading! Good luck!
